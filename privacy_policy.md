We may collect the following types of information:

Personally Identifiable Information (PII):
Full name

Email address

Phone number

Mailing address (if needed)

Payment information (processed by secure third-party payment service providers—we do not store your payment details)

Unique user ID (assigned by the App)

App Usage Information:
Login data (date, time, duration of use)

Features used within the App

Pages visited

Device information (device type, operating system, device ID)

App diagnostic and performance data

Subscription Information:
Type of subscription (general electricity, water and plumbing, refrigeration and air conditioning)

Subscription status (active, expired, canceled)

Payment history

This data may be used to personalize certain services, connect you with local professionals (if this feature is available), or for aggregated statistical purposes. You may revoke your consent at any time through your device settings.

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

Create and manage your user account

Process subscriptions and payments

Provide customer support

To Improve the App and Our Services:
Analyze app usage to identify improvements, optimize user experience, and develop new features

Conduct research and data analysis on service usage trends

Personalize your experience based on your subscription and usage

To Communicate With You:
Send important information regarding the App, updates, or changes to our policies

Send promotional offers and updates about our services and related services in electricity, water, plumbing, and HVAC (with your consent where required)

Respond to your inquiries and requests

To Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud:
Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity on the App and services

Protect our systems and your information

To Fulfill Legal Obligations:
Respond to lawful requests by authorities

Exercise our rights and defend against legal claims

3. Sharing Your Information
We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:

Third-Party Service Providers:
We work with third-party companies to help deliver and improve our services, including payment processing, data hosting, app analytics, marketing, customer support, and potentially subscription services (e.g., connecting with technicians if applicable). These providers have limited access to your information and are contractually obligated to protect it and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Business Partners (with your consent):
If we offer joint services or promotions with other companies in the fields of electricity, water, plumbing, or HVAC, we may share your data with them, but only with your explicit consent.

Legal Authorities:
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., court order or subpoena).

Business Transfers:
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, your personal data may be transferred to the acquiring party. You will be informed of any change in ownership or control of your data.

Aggregated and Anonymized Data:
We may share aggregated and anonymized data that does not directly identify you with third parties for analysis, marketing, or other purposes.

4. Your Rights Regarding Your Personal Data
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): In certain cases, you can request the deletion of your personal data.

Right to Restrict Processing: You may request limitations on how we process your personal data in specific circumstances.

Right to Object: You may object to the use of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format and transmit it to another data controller.

Right to Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the “Contact Us” section below. We will respond in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. The specific retention period may vary depending on the type of data and purpose of processing. For example, subscription data may be retained beyond the active period for historical or legal purposes.

7. International Data Transfers
Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws.
We implement appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected during such transfers, in compliance with applicable laws. This may include the use of standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities.

8. Children’s Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 17.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information without verified parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.
If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
